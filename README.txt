EMB 121 Xingu

Utility Aircraft

The EMBRAER EMB 121 Xingu is a twin-engined utility and corporate aircraft for up to 9 passengers produced by the Brazilian manufacturer Embraer (Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica, S.A.).
